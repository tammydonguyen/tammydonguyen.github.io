Look at the solutions I've provided for `attach` and `append`. What are the tail calls? Are the functions tail recursive? <blockquote class="solution-4">`attach` is not tail recursive. The tail calls are a call to `list` and a call to `cons`. The tail call for `append` is to `cons`. This is also not tail recursive, since the recursive call is passed as an argument to `cons`. </blockquote> <a class="solution-toggle-4 btn btn-default">TOGGLE SOLUTION</a> 2.
